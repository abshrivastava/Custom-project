How Carepoynt Works

We're creating a rewarding new approach to consumer-centric healthcare with a powerful and personalized program, an advanced care planning and poynts based technology platform, and an extensive network of health and wellness partners

Connect & Align

  • Joyn Us > Connect and create your free Carepoynt member account
  • Align your healthy lifestyle interests with CarePlans, partners and promotions
  • Make and attend your doctor and other wellness appointments
  • Shop for health and wellness items within the Carepoynt Community

Engage & Earn

  • Learn about exciting Health and Wellness partners and promotions
  • Earn CarePoynts for Engaging in healthy activities, health and wellness purchases, promos & more
  • Refer friends and invite your family to become part of your CareClique > everyone earns Poynts for Joyning!
  • Share feedback in the 'app' or Social media reviews online > More Poynts!

Redeem & Share

  • Redeem CarePoynts for health-related products, services or experiences from Carepoynt Community providers and partners
  • Save up, pool and even share your Poynts within your CareClique!
  • Participate in or donate to a 'Share the Care' cause or event. Help a loved one or surprise them with a Reward
